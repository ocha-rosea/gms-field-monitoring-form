How it works

1 Export

Before departure, export the project's monitoring template from OneGMS, one Excel file per project.

2 Load

Open this page on a phone, tablet or laptop and drop the file in. After the first visit the page opens even with no connection, and you can add it to your home screen.

3 Fill in the field

Short guided steps with the official dropdowns, scoring rubrics and mandatory-field checks. Drafts save automatically.

4 Generate & upload

One click regenerates the official GMS Excel with your answers filled in. Review it and upload to OneGMS as usual.

The file uploaded to OneGMS is always the official template. This form only fills it in.

  • Nothing is sent anywhere. Everything you type is kept only in this browser, on this device. The app has no server. The only output is the Excel file you download and upload to OneGMS yourself.
  • Refreshing or closing the page is safe. A draft is saved automatically on this device after every change. Reopen the page (it loads even with no connection after the first visit), load the same template file, and your entries are restored.
  • Changing device or browser? Use Export draft to save your entries as a small file, and Import draft on the other device. The generated Excel also carries all your answers, so re-loading it restores them too.
  • The draft stays until you press Discard draft or clear this browser's site data. Private/incognito windows forget drafts when closed, so avoid them for field work.
  • For official UN use. Use organization-issued devices, and download or share exported files through internal channels only, to protect the personal data in monitoring reports.
